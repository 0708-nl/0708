About 0708

0708 is an underground hard techno and hardcore artist focused on raw energy, heavy percussion, and uncompromising sound. Rooted in late-night intensity and dark-room atmosphere, the project brings together distorted basslines, aggressive drums, and a direct, physical approach that stands out in the modern electronic music scene.

The sound of 0708 is built for high-pressure sets, intense club environments, and audiences looking for something direct, powerful, and unmistakably underground. Whether delivering a fierce live set or crafting new material, 0708 stays true to a bold, relentless identity.
