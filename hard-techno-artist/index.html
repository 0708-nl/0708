Hard Techno Artist

0708 is a hard techno artist and hardcore producer creating dark, heavy, and uncompromising electronic music. The project is shaped by sharp drums, driving bass, and a pure underground attitude that connects with club audiences and lovers of powerful, high-energy sound.

From raw kicks to relentless structure, 0708 delivers music that feels immediate and physical. It is made for the late-night crowd, the dark room, and the kind of set where intensity matters more than polish.
