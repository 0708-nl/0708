About 0708

0708 delivers hard, distorted, high-pressure electronic music built for dark rooms, late hours, and relentless systems. Focused on raw kicks, hardcore energy, and no-frills underground intensity, 0708 keeps the sound direct, heavy, and physical.

For Promoters

Promoter information

Venue fit

Heavy rigs and sharp sound are required. Confirm PA specs, power, and load-in windows before the show is locked.

Show timing

Late sets work best. Keep the slot tight and build the right crowd for a raw, uncompromising performance.

Promotion

Use underground hard techno imagery, not festival gloss. The audience is looking for intensity, not pop polish.

Payment

Email contact@0708.nl to confirm deposit, rider, and contract terms before the show.
