Real World Problems

Real World Problems

Booking logistics

Last-minute load-ins, conflicting schedules, mismatched tech — solves need clear communication and firm riders.

Power & sound

Weak or noisy PA systems, unstable power, and strict curfews kill sets; venues must confirm specs in advance.

Rider expectations

Mismatch between rider and in-house gear causes delays — always confirm console, cables, and input lists.

Crowd & safety

Overcapacity, poor ventilation, or lack of security create unsafe shows — these are non-negotiable issues.

Promotion & payment

Late promotion, cancelled shows, or delayed payments disrupt touring — deposits and clear contracts protect everyone.

If you need help resolving any of these, contact booking@0708.example
