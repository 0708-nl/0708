About 0708

0708 is an underground hard techno and hardcore artist known for raw kicks, brutal energy, and direct late-night sound. The project focuses on heavy drums, intense atmosphere, and uncompromising sets for dark rooms, high-pressure crowds, and relentless systems across the electronic music scene.
